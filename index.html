About Me

I am a versatile Game Developer and Front-End Web Developer with a strong foundation in C++. In the realm of game development, I specialize in using GLUT and SFML to create interactive and visually compelling games. My expertise includes implementing game mechanics, rendering graphics, and optimizing performance to deliver smooth and immersive gaming experiences. As a Front-End Developer, I have a deep understanding of HTML and CSS, with a particular focus on CSS animations. I excel at bringing web designs to life, creating responsive, dynamic, and visually appealing websites. My ability to blend technical skills in both game development and front-end design allows me to craft engaging experiences across different digital platforms.
